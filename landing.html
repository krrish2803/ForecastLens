FAQ

Questions we hear every day

How accurate are the forecasts?▾

Our Prophet-based engine achieves 94% median accuracy on P50 forecasts when validated against holdout periods. More importantly, the P10-P90 confidence bands give you a realistic range — so clients understand the uncertainty. No forecast is 100% accurate, but a probability range is infinitely more useful than a single number.

Which ad platforms do you support?▾

Google Ads, Meta Ads, Microsoft Ads (Bing), and any platform that exports to CSV with date/spend/revenue columns. We also support GA4 and Shopify exports. The pipeline auto-detects the platform from column naming conventions and normalizes everything into a unified schema.

How do you handle attribution gaps (e.g., Bing Revenue = $0)?▾

We automatically detect when a channel has >80% zero-revenue rows. In that case, we apply a click-based revenue proxy using the median revenue-per-click from non-zero rows. This prevents crashes and produces usable forecasts. The proxy is flagged in the output — you'll always know when data quality is affecting the forecast.

Can non-technical team members use this?▾

Absolutely. The pipeline runs with one command: ./run.sh. The web dashboard requires no technical knowledge — upload CSVs, click a button, get forecasts and AI summaries. Designed for account managers and media buyers, not just data scientists.

What's the difference between P10, P50, and P90?▾

P50 is the median forecast — the midpoint of probable outcomes. P10 is the 10th percentile (only a 10% chance revenue will be lower). P90 is the 90th percentile (90% chance revenue will be lower). Think of it as: conservative, expected, and optimistic scenarios. Our 80% prediction interval means actual results should fall between P10 and P90 about 80% of the time.

Do I need an API key for the AI summary?▾

Yes — the AI causal summary uses Anthropic's Claude. Set ANTHROPIC_API_KEY in your environment. Without it, the dashboard shows a fallback text. All forecasting and simulation features work without any API key.
